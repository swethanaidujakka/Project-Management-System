ABOUT THE PROJECT


This page contains information regarding the miniproject.In every project there are five users.They are Administrator, Coordinator, Guide, TeamLeader, TeamMember.Administrator monitor everything related to project.Coordinator manage and maintains everything and post the deadlines and view the marks which are posted by guides.Guide approves the project and post marks of their teams.TeamLeader post status and view status.TeamMember view status.Administrator,Coordinator,Guide no need to register,They have own login credentials.TeamLeader,TeamMember need to register.
